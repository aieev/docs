AirCloud is a fully distributed AI cloud platform designed to deploy and operate AI inference workloads quickly and flexibly. Users can either integrate models quickly through APIs or deploy applications and runtimes directly as containers to build the environment they need.

From simple prototyping to production services, AirCloud provides multiple starting points based on different service goals and operating models. If you are new to AirCloud, we recommend first choosing the right starting point between Air API and Air Container, then evaluating AirCloud or AirCloud+ as infrastructure options based on your service requirements.

## Infrastructure options

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="AirCloud" icon="cloud">
    A distributed infrastructure option for workloads where cost efficiency and flexibility matter most. It is a good fit for teams looking to run AI inference workloads at a practical cost.
  </Card>

  <Card title="AirCloud+" icon="shield-check">
    A dedicated infrastructure option for workloads that require stronger stability, performance, availability, and operational control. It is a good fit for enterprise environments and critical production workloads.

    <Note>
      🛡️ ISO 27001 Certified — a strong fit for enterprise-grade security and compliance requirements.
    </Note>
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Which option should you choose?

### Choose your deployment method

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="For faster integration" icon="rocket">
    [Air API](/docs/air-api) is the right choice if you want to integrate high-quality open-source model APIs quickly and reduce operational overhead.
  </Card>

  <Card title="For deeper control" icon="settings">
    [Air Container](/docs/air-container) is the right choice if you want to manage container images, runtimes, dependencies, and service configurations directly.
  </Card>
</Columns>

### Choose your infrastructure option

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="For cost efficiency" icon="wallet">
    AirCloud is the right choice if you need flexible and cost-efficient infrastructure for AI inference workloads.
  </Card>

  <Card title="For stronger reliability" icon="badge-check">
    AirCloud+ is the right choice if you need higher stability, more predictable performance, and a more dedicated operating environment.
  </Card>
</Columns>
