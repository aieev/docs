This page summarizes the infrastructure options and GPU instance types available in AirCloud.

## Infrastructure options

AirCloud offers two infrastructure options. Choose based on your workload's requirements for stability and cost.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="AirCloud Plus" icon="shield-check">
    A dedicated infrastructure option operated directly by AIEEV. Well suited for workloads requiring stable, predictable performance. ISO 27001 certified.
  </Card>

  <Card title="AirCloud Zero" icon="cloud">
    A distributed infrastructure option leveraging crowdsourced GPU resources. Well suited for cost-efficient workloads. (Coming soon)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## GPU instance types

The following GPU instances are available on AirCloud Plus. Choose based on your model's VRAM requirements.

| Instance | VRAM | RAM | vCPU |
|---|---|---|---|
| RTX 4070 Super | 12 GB | 9 GB | 8 |
| RTX 4070 Ti Super | 16 GB | 58 GB | 8 |
| RTX 4090 | 24 GB | 58 GB | 8 |
| RTX 5090 | 32 GB | 58 GB | 16 |
| RTX PRO 6000 | 96 GB | 58 GB | 16 |

## Choosing the right VRAM for your model

Use the table below as a general guide when selecting an instance.

| Model size | Recommended VRAM |
|---|---|
| 7B | 12 GB+ |
| 13B | 16 GB+ |
| 30B | 24 GB+ |
| 70B | 32 GB+ |
| Multimodal | 96 GB |

## Pricing

Pricing varies by instance type. Long-term rental discounts (3 months or more) are available. See the [pricing page](https://www.aieev.com/en/pricing) for the latest rates.
