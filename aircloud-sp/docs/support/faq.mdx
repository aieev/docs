## What should I do first to use Aircloud?

Sign up and log in, then create an Organization and a Project. After that, start with either Air API or Air Container depending on your workflow.

## Are credits required?

Credits may appear optional during onboarding, but we recommend confirming billing/credits readiness before running real workloads.

## Can I view a secret key again?

No. Secret keys cannot be viewed again after creation. If you lose it, generate a new key.

## Why isn’t my container running immediately after deployment?

Right after deployment, a container may appear as **Pending**. Pending state is not billed, and you must click **Start container** to run workloads.

## What’s the difference between Autoscaling and Scheduled Scaling?

Autoscaling adjusts replicas based on conditions, while Scheduled Scaling adjusts replicas on a time-based schedule.
